## Getting Started

### Download the App

**Android:**
- Google Play Store: Search "AgriSupply Uganda"

**iOS:**
- App Store: Search "AgriSupply Uganda"

### Create an Account

1. Open the AgriSupply app
2. Tap **Register**
3. Choose your role:
   - **Buyer** - Purchase fresh produce
   - **Farmer** - Sell your farm products
4. Fill in your details:
   - Full name
   - Phone number (MTN or Airtel)
   - Email address
   - Password (at least 8 characters)
   - Region (Central, Eastern, Western, Northern)
5. Verify your phone number with the OTP code
6. Start using AgriSupply!

### Login

1. Open the app
2. Enter your email and password
3. Tap **Login**

If you forget your password:
1. Tap **Forgot Password**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions

## For Buyers

### Browsing Products

**Home Screen:**
- Featured products appear at the top
- Browse by category (Vegetables, Fruits, Dairy, etc.)
- See products from your region

**Search:**
1. Tap the search icon
2. Enter product name or keywords
3. Use filters:
   - **Category**: Select product type
   - **Price Range**: Set min/max price
   - **Rating**: Filter by star rating
   - **Organic Only**: Show only organic products
   - **Region**: Products from specific areas

### Viewing Product Details

Tap any product to see:
- High-quality images
- Detailed description
- Price per unit (kg, piece, bunch, etc.)
- Farmer information and rating
- Available quantity
- Customer reviews

### Adding to Cart

1. On product detail screen, tap **Add to Cart**
2. Select quantity using +/- buttons
3. Tap **Add to Cart**
4. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

### Managing Your Cart

1. Tap the cart icon in the bottom navigation
2. View all items in your cart
3. Adjust quantities or remove items
4. See subtotal and estimated delivery cost
5. Tap **Proceed to Checkout**

### Checkout

1. **Delivery Address:**
   - Select saved address or add new one
   - Enter: Street, Area, District, Landmark

2. **Delivery Notes:**
   - Add special instructions (e.g., "Call on arrival")

3. **Payment Method:**
   - MTN Mobile Money
   - Airtel Money
   - Card Payment
   - Cash on Delivery (available in some areas)

4. **Review Order:**
   - Check items and total
   - Tap **Place Order**

### Tracking Orders

1. Go to **My Orders** from bottom navigation
2. See all your orders with status:
   - **Pending**: Waiting for farmer confirmation
   - **Confirmed**: Farmer accepted your order
   - **Processing**: Being prepared
   - **Shipped**: On the way to you
   - **Delivered**: Complete!

3. Tap any order for details:
   - Order items
   - Farmer contact
   - Delivery address
   - Payment status

### Leaving Reviews

After receiving your order:
1. Go to **My Orders**
2. Find the delivered order
3. Tap **Leave Review**
4. Rate 1-5 stars
5. Write your experience
6. Submit

## For Farmers

### Getting Started as a Farmer

1. Complete your farmer profile:
   - Farm name
   - Farm location
   - Description of your farm
   - Profile photo

2. Get verified (optional but recommended):
   - Upload farm photos
   - Provide location details
   - Admin will review and verify

### Adding Products

1. From dashboard, tap **Add Product**
2. Fill in product details:
   - **Name**: Clear product name
   - **Category**: Select appropriate category
   - **Description**: Detailed description
   - **Price**: Price per unit
   - **Unit**: kg, piece, bunch, litre, etc.
   - **Quantity**: Available stock
   - **Organic**: Toggle if organic
3. Add photos (up to 5)
4. Tap **Publish**

Products are reviewed before going live.

### Managing Products

1. Go to **My Products** from dashboard
2. See all your listed products
3. Tap any product to:
   - Edit details
   - Update quantity
   - Set as out of stock
   - Delete listing

### Handling Orders

When you receive an order:
1. You'll get a notification
2. Go to **Orders** from dashboard
3. View order details:
   - Customer info
   - Delivery address
   - Items ordered
   - Total amount

4. **Confirm** or **Reject** the order
5. Update status as you prepare:
   - Mark as **Processing**
   - Mark as **Shipped**
   - Mark as **Delivered**

### Dashboard Analytics

Your dashboard shows:
- **Total Sales**: Earnings this month
- **Orders**: Number of orders
- **Products**: Active listings
- **Rating**: Your average rating

### AI Farming Assistant

Access intelligent farming advice:
1. Tap **AI Assistant** from dashboard
2. Ask questions about:
   - Crop diseases and treatments
   - Best planting times
   - Pest control methods
   - Weather-based recommendations

**Crop Analysis:**
1. Take a photo of your crops
2. Upload for AI analysis
3. Get instant feedback on:
   - Plant health
   - Disease detection
   - Treatment recommendations

### Premium Features

Upgrade to Premium for:
- Featured product placement
- Priority in search results
- Unlimited product listings
- Advanced analytics
- Priority customer support

## Payments

### Supported Payment Methods

**MTN Mobile Money:**
1. Select MTN MoMo at checkout
2. Enter your MTN number (256 77...)
3. Confirm the amount
4. Approve payment on your phone
5. Enter your PIN when prompted

**Airtel Money:**
1. Select Airtel Money at checkout
2. Enter your Airtel number (256 70...)
3. Confirm the amount
4. Approve payment on your phone
5. Enter your PIN when prompted

**Card Payment:**
1. Select Card at checkout
2. Enter card details
3. Complete 3D Secure verification

**Cash on Delivery:**
- Available in select areas
- Pay in cash when you receive your order

### Payment Security

- All transactions are encrypted
- We never store your full card details
- Mobile Money payments are processed securely
- Transaction receipts sent via SMS

### Refunds

If there's an issue with your order:
1. Contact the farmer first
2. If unresolved, contact support
3. Refunds are processed to original payment method
4. Mobile Money refunds: 1-3 business days
5. Card refunds: 5-10 business days

## FAQ

**Q: How do I contact a farmer?**
A: From the product page, tap the farmer's profile to see contact options.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: Orders can be cancelled before the farmer confirms. After confirmation, contact the farmer directly.

**Q: What areas do you deliver to?**
A: Delivery is arranged between you and the farmer. Most farmers deliver within their region.

**Q: How fresh is the produce?**
A: Products are sourced directly from farms. Freshness depends on the individual farmer.

**Q: Is there a minimum order?**
A: Each farmer sets their own minimum order requirements.

**Q: How do farmers get paid?**
A: Farmers receive payment directly to their Mobile Money account after successful delivery.

**Q: What if my order is damaged?**
A: Contact support within 24 hours with photos of the damage. We'll help resolve the issue.
